About Cosmic shop

Welcome to Cosmic Books, a little corner filled with the stories I've fallen in love with. Each book here was chosen the way you'd choose a close friend to bring into your home. They're the ones I've laughed with, cried with, and been thrilled by. My hope is that you'll find a book here that just 'clicks', and that it becomes a favorite friend on your own shelf.
